Mental Health Tools

Know yourself.
Heal yourself.

MEND's self-evaluation tools give you real insight into your emotional state — quickly, privately, and without judgment.

Adapted for African youth using validated tools like PHQ-9 and GAD-7, localised for cultural relevance.

deserve a canvas.

Storytelling & Writing

Journaling prompts, narrative therapy exercises, and a personal story archive you can publish or keep private.

Visual Art & Digital Drawing
