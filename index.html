About MEND

Built for Africa, by Africans

MEND (Mental and Emotional Nurturing Digital) is a youth empowerment platform developed at African Leadership University. We address the mental health crisis affecting young people across Africa by combining therapy, creativity, and career development.

Our platform is built on the belief that mental wellbeing and personal growth are inseparable — and that every young African deserves access to both.

Our Mission
  • 🎯Reduce mental health stigma among African youth
  • 🌍Provide culturally sensitive digital mental health services
  • 💡Foster creative self-expression and talent development
  • 🚀Empower youth with career guidance and mentorship
